Research

  • AU-driven Stable Diffusion: Optimized action unit driven stable diffusion based on InstructPix2Pix backbone on self-generated high-quality face pair datasets. Supervised by Babak Taati, University Health Network.
  • DeepEmotion: Led a team of 8 to build scalable and interpretable emotional response diagnostic tools from 7T fMRI for mental healthcare. Supervised by Jurgen Germann, UTMIST.
  • UNet for KRD: Proposed a novel architecture for medical image segmentation of knee recess distension. Supervised by Pascal Tyrrell, MiDATA Lab.
  • Neurologically Plausible Neural Networks: Simulated human brain sensorimotor functions through artificial neural networks and EEG data. Supervised by Mattias Niemeier, CoNSens Lab.
